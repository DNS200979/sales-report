🎯 Conclusion – Pure ACS (No Embedded Agent)

✅ Aprecomm VCS leads in modernity (TR-369 USP), mass management (Policy Engine), and advanced diagnostics (TR-143). Disadvantage: less friendly usability for L1 and the need for custom integration with Brazilian ERPs.
✅ Beegol is a strong modern contender with good visual dashboards for L1, though slightly behind Anlix in pure native integrations for the local market without its agent active.
✅ Anlix (Flashman) stands out for exceptional usability for L1 and native integrations with ERPs in Brazil. Disadvantage: based only on TR-069 (polling), no automatic rollback.
⚠️ Tier 3 (IXC/Made4Graph/INT6) offers cost-effective solutions for ISPs. IXC and INT6 are perfect if you already use their respective ERPs, while Made4Graph offers superior UI/UX for L1, although all lack advanced protocols (TR-369) and massive scalability without bottlenecks.

🧠 Conclusion – With Embedded Agent Capabilities

Aprecomm (VWE) and Beegol (Agent) are the platforms that truly offer an Edge AI ecosystem for ISP networks by utilizing embedded agents. They deliver predictive maintenance, self-healing Wi-Fi, application-level QoE, and automated diagnostics. This translates directly into a reduction of support calls and increased satisfaction.
Anlix and Made4Graph offer excellent visual aids and dashboards, but their operation remains essentially reactive based on polling, requiring human interpretation.
Tier 3 (IXC/INT6) does not offer edge intelligence or visual abstractions, relying purely on manual parameter checks.
